What we detect

Three models.
One extension.

Every email is analysed in parallel by three purpose-built ML models, each specialised in a different threat vector.

Phishing Email Detector
Logistic Regression trained on 100k+ emails. Detects urgency language, spoofed domains, header anomalies, and malicious link patterns with LIME-powered evidence.
TF-IDF · Handcrafted features
Malicious URL Classifier
XGBoost with 400 trees classifying URLs into phishing, malware, defacement, or benign across 36 extracted features including brand impersonation and TLD risk.
XGBoost · 36 features
Prompt Injection Detector
Detects 5 attack types: direct override, role play, data exfiltration, jailbreak, and indirect injection using trigram TF-IDF plus 12 structural behavioural features.
Trigram · 5 attack types
Auto-Block Senders
Senders scoring CRITICAL on 3 or more emails are automatically quarantined. Desktop notifications fire instantly. One-click unblock from the dashboard.
Auto-quarantine
X-Mailer Detection
Identifies PHP-class mailers, bulk senders, and spoofed mail clients from X-Mailer headers. Adds a risk penalty and flags the specific mailer type in the dashboard.
Header analysis
100% Local Processing
All inference runs on your machine via a local Flask server. No email content ever leaves your device. GPT-4o-mini summaries are optional and separately opted in.
